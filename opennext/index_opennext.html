Case studies in the Digital Fabrication of Open-Source Consumer Electronic Products

Background.

Digital fabrication is challenging the assumptions that underly mass production. It enables individual variation.
There are numerous examples of small businesses using digital fabrication for the production of consumer products:
- Freedom of Creation, 3d print for lamps, furniture, and personal accessories. (3D Systems acquired Freedom of Creation in May of 2011)
- Nervous System, jewelry and housewares, 3D printers, laser cutters, and other fabrication processes
- Wood Marvels, wooden toys composed of laser cut wood, using Ponoko as a fabrication service and showroom
Particularly when an outside service, like Ponoko, is used for the production, digital fabrication allows these companies to start with a minimum of start-up capital or investment.
Electronic production: circuit board fabrication is a mature and widespread digital fabrication technology.
